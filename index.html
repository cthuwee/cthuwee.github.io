Origins

In the Eons of Aldebaran, the great Cthulhu was struck by loss when his firstborn vanished, taken by the mysterious Shadows of Yog-Sothoth.
Fueled by wrath and love, Cthulhu prepared to shake the heavens in search. But first, he entrusted his remaining offspring, the cuddly Cthuwees, into the loving arms of humans.
Adopt an adorable Cthuwee today, and share a cosmic bond of love that bridges worlds, as you wait for Cthulhu's triumphant return.
